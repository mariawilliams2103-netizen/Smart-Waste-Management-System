🌱 About SWMS

What is SWMS?

The Smart Waste Management System (SWMS) is a technology-driven platform designed to modernize and optimize waste collection, sorting, and disposal processes across urban and rural communities.

By leveraging IoT sensors, real-time data analytics, and a user-friendly mobile/web interface, SWMS ensures timely collection, reduces operational costs, and promotes recycling and environmental sustainability.

⚠️ The Problem

Challenges in Waste Management

Traditional waste management systems are inefficient, costly, and environmentally damaging. Here are the key problems we solve.

🗑️

Overflowing Bins

Bins overflow before scheduled collection, causing health hazards and environmental pollution in communities.

🚛

Inefficient Routes

Collectors follow fixed routes regardless of bin fill levels, wasting fuel and time on unnecessary trips.

📋

Poor Tracking

No real-time visibility of collection status leads to missed pickups and resident complaints going unresolved.

💰

High Operational Costs

Manual processes and unoptimized scheduling drive up operational costs significantly for service providers.

♻️

Low Recycling Rates

Lack of awareness and poor waste sorting infrastructure leads to very low recycling and high landfill usage.

📞

No Communication

Residents have no easy way to report issues, request pickups, or receive updates about collection schedules.

🔄 Process

How It Works

A simple four-step process that connects residents, collectors, and managers seamlessly.

1

Register & Setup

Residents register on the platform, set up their profile, and link their address to the nearest waste collection zone.

2

Monitor & Detect

IoT sensors in smart bins detect fill levels and automatically trigger collection requests when thresholds are reached.

3

Dispatch & Collect

The system optimizes routes and dispatches the nearest available collector to efficiently complete the pickup.

4

Report & Improve

Collection data is logged, analyzed, and used to continuously improve routes, schedules, and service quality.
